MUSE WRITE

Writing Guidelines

1. Story Structure

Every story should have a clear beginning, middle, and end. Follow these key elements:

  • Hook your readers in the first paragraph
  • Establish the main conflict early
  • Develop character arcs throughout the story
  • Build tension progressively
  • Provide a satisfying resolution
Example Opening:

"The day the sky turned green was the day Sarah realized she could no longer pretend everything was normal."

2. Character Development

Do:

  • Give characters clear motivations
  • Show character growth
  • Create distinct voices

Don't:

  • Rely on stereotypes
  • Make characters static
  • Tell instead of show

3. Dialogue Writing

Effective dialogue serves multiple purposes:

  • Reveals character personality
  • Moves the plot forward
  • Creates tension or conflict
  • Provides necessary information naturally
Strong Dialogue Example:

"I told you to leave it alone," Mark said, his fingers drumming against the table. "But you never listen, do you?"

4. World Building

  • Create consistent rules for your world
  • Introduce elements gradually
  • Show the world through character interactions
  • Balance description with action

5. Content Standards

  • Maintain appropriate content for your target audience
  • Use content warnings when necessary
  • Avoid gratuitous violence or explicit content
  • Respect diverse perspectives and cultures
